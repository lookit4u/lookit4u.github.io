The lookit4u website opened in mid-2019, the first goal was to find products, ones that sell better and ones that sell less, and test and review the same products.

Once it was done, the idea was to distribute it online to help anyone hesitant to buy and purchase the product to make the right decision for themselves.

At the moment you can find a unique focus on individual products. If you would like us to review any product you have found, feel free to contact us.
