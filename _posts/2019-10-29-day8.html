I won't lie, I was a little disappointed to see that I couldn't lose weight from yesterday to today, indeed it is not wise to measure your weight every day, but I really wanted to see the changes, like a little research on my body.

Weight - 95.5kg

Like I said yesterday, the workout I did was very massive and focused mainly on the chest. And I didn't do cardio like I did two days ago, it might have an effect, but if my muscle mass increased and then my body weight increased then I think it's fine.

Anyway, I feel good and not too hungry, which is already good, and besides, I gained weight only 0.200kg so I guess it's really okay to see a slight increase every once in awhile, but that means that I'm not sure I'll reach 91.8kg this weekend.

At the moment, despite the slight weight gain it still seems to be declining, for me it is excellent. The Bar Brothers program does help me lose weight and their diet plan helps me stay calm and not hungry.
