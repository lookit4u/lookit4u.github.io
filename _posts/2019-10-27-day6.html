I've eaten what I always eat after training, Sesame sauce with tuna and two eggs, let's me write you how to make the tahini from Sesame sauce, it's very tasty.

Ingredients:
1 cup of sesame sauce
2 crushed garlic cloves
3/4 cup of cold water
1/2 lemon squeezed medium
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup chopped parsley (not must)

preparation method:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Keep in the fridge.
The tahini can be accredited by adding a little tahini or diluting by adding a little water.
